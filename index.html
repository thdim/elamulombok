A Slice of Greece in Lombok

Authentic Greek cuisine, warm hospitality, and stunning island ambiance.

At Elamu Lombok authentic Greek cooking meets the beauty of Lombok. It takes a mother's recipes and a menu crafted with love. Heartful hospitality, for you to feel at home. And a Mediterranean House made for hanging out all times of the day and evening.

Kalos irthate!

Menu Highlights

Breakfast

Sourdough toast with salmon and avocado, eggs Benedict, and traditional Greek pastries—perfectly paired with excellent coffee.

Lunch & Dinner

Home-style dishes like moussaka, grilled favorites including pita souvlaki, vibrant Greek salads, and unforgettable seafood.

Dessert

Baklava rolls, phyllo-wrapped fried halloumi drizzled with honey, and other indulgent treats.

Drinks

Classic cocktails and signature twists, infused spirits, fresh juices, iced teas, and expertly brewed coffee—from espresso to cappuccino.
